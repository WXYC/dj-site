# `library_identity.entity_id` is the canonical cross-app artist identifier

The cross-app canonical artist identifier across Backend-Service, semantic-index, the iOS DJ tool, this app, and future WXYC surfaces is `library_identity.entity_id` as composed by LML. For v1 we (like iOS) continue to use BS `artists.id` because the `library_identity` substrate is currently empty in production (depends on [BS#802](https://github.com/WXYC/Backend-Service/issues/802) and [LML project #25 cross-cache-identity](https://github.com/orgs/WXYC/projects/25)) — when the substrate lights up, BS swaps its internal identifier translation under us and we keep shipping unchanged.

The practical near-term lift on our side is small: we already get `artist.id` through join paths in the catalog results, but if and when [`wxyc-shared/api.yaml`](https://github.com/WXYC/wxyc-shared/blob/main/api.yaml) `AlbumSearchResult` grows an explicit `artist_id` field (cross-repo coordination item C1 — iOS needs this to enable Artist Deep Dive), we can pick it up from there rather than synthesizing it client-side. Homonym artists ("Beach" the Korean band vs the German artist) remain ambiguous in our deep links until the substrate is wired.

Canonical source: [`wxyc-dj-tool-ios/docs/cross-repo-adrs.md` ADR 0001](https://github.com/WXYC/wxyc-dj-tool-ios/blob/main/docs/cross-repo-adrs.md#adr-0001--library_identityentity_id-is-the-canonical-artist-identifier) and the repo-local [iOS ADR 0001](https://github.com/WXYC/wxyc-dj-tool-ios/blob/main/docs/adr/0001-entity-id-canonical-artist-identifier.md).
